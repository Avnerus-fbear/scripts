# Turku & Muumimaailma Trip Plan

**When:** Friday June 19 – Sunday June 21, 2026
**Who:** Avner + 2 kids (4yo & 2yo)
**Created:** 2026-06-19

---

## Day 1 — Friday June 19: Turku City Exploration

### Morning: Old Town & Castle Area

- **[Turku Castle](https://turunlinna.fi/en/home-page/)** — Kids love castles! Family-friendly atmosphere, exhibitions, and a great courtyard for running around. Castle restaurant good for late breakfast/brunch.
  - 📍 Linnankatu 80 | ⏰ Tue–Sun 10:00–18:00 (summer) | 💰 Adults €18, children 7–15 €8, under 7 free, family ticket €44
  - ⚠️ **Closed June 19–21 (Midsummer holidays)** — [source](https://turunlinna.fi/en/opening-times-and-prices/). Skip today, visit Sunday evening or another day.
  - 💡 The courtyard is free to access even when the museum is closed

- **[Luostarinmäki Museum Quarter](https://luostarinmaki.fi/en/)** — Historic wooden houses with craftspeople live at work (glassblowing, blacksmith, etc.). Very sensory and engaging for young kids. Narrow streets to explore.
  - 📍 Vartiovuorenkatu 2 | ⏰ Summer Mon–Sun 10:00–18:00 | 💰 Adults €12, children 7–15 €5, under 7 free, family ticket €29
  - Opening hours & prices: [luostarinmaki.fi/en/opening-times-and-prices](https://luostarinmaki.fi/en/opening-times-and-prices/)
  - 💡 Watch the glassblowing — toddlers find it mesmerizing

- **[Turku Market Square](https://www.turku.fi/en/families-children)** (Kauppatori) — Fresh produce, street food, ice cream. Let the kids pick something. Great atmosphere in summer.
  - 📍 Aurakatu 1 | 💰 Free to browse
  - 💡 Grab fishcakes or ice cream from the stalls

### Lunch

- **[Turun Kauppahalli](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g189949-d3164688-Reviews-Turun_Kauppahalli-Turku_Southwest_Finland.html)** (Turku Market Hall) — Indoor food market since 1896 with multiple vendors. S. Wallin's fish shop is legendary. Plenty of options for picky eaters.
  - 📍 Rauhankatu 2 | ⏰ Mon–Sat 08:00–16:00, Sun 08:00–14:00

### Afternoon: River & Islands

- **[Ispoinen Beach](https://www.suomi.fi/services/servicepoint/ispoinen-beach-city-of-turku/438d4ed9-8df5-4025-9667-fb6449e940af)** — Child-friendly sand beach, free. Has changing area, toilets, sauna, kiosk, children's play equipment. Lifeguard-supervised summer.
  - 📍 Rykmentintie 51 | 💰 Free | ⏰ Lifeguard Mon–Sun 11:00–18:00 (Jun 1 – Aug 15)
  - 💡 Bring swimsuits! Shallow sandy beach ideal for toddlers

- **[Saaronniemi Beach, Ruissalo](https://www.suomi.fi/services/servicepoint/saaronniemi-beach-city-of-turku/9a1e5b31-df7f-4916-8da4-0dab4055debb)** — Child-friendly sand beach on Ruissalo island, free. Has changing rooms, toilets, shower, children's playground, beach volleyball, bookable sauna. Reachable by free summer ferry from Market Square.
  - 📍 Saarontie 59, Ruissalo | 💰 Free
  - 💡 The free ferry ride from Market Square is fun for kids too

- **[Föri Ferry](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/fori-ferry)** — The free ferry crossing the Aura River. Kids love boats! Runs continuously, completely free.
  - 📍 Departs from Market Square side | 💰 Free

- **[Botanical Garden, University of Turku](https://sites.utu.fi/kasvitieteellinen-puutarha/en/)** — Beautiful gardens with a tropical greenhouse. The tropical house is magical for little ones — exotic plants, humid air, colorful flowers.
  - 📍 Lemminkäisenkatu 22, Ruissalo | ⏰ Greenhouse Mon–Tue & Thu–Sun 11:00–17:00, Wed 11:00–19:00; Outdoor garden daily 08:00–20:00
  - 💰 Greenhouse: Adults €12, young 7–17 €6, under 7 free, family ticket €28. Outdoor gardens free.
  - 💡 The greenhouse is the highlight for toddlers

### Evening

- **[Aurachannel promenade](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/aurachannel-promenade)** walk — Beautiful summer evening, street performers sometimes around
- Dinner in Old Town area — several family-friendly restaurants near the castle

## Day 2 — Saturday June 20: Muumimaailma (Moominworld) 🌟

**This is THE day.** Midsummer celebration at the park!

### Getting There

- **By car:** ~20 min from Turku center (16 km)
- **By boat:** Summer boat connection from Turku — very scenic and fun for kids
- **By bus:** Föli bus from Turku
- 📍 Kailon saari, Naantali
- More info: [moominworld.fi](https://www.moominworld.fi/)

### ⭐ Midsummer Special (June 19–20)

You're visiting during **Moomin Family's Midsummer Dance** — extra special!
- **2:00 PM** — Grand Midsummer Dance at Moominhouse (communal singing & dancing)
- Special Midsummer ice cream (€7)
- Pancakes with jam, strawberries, whipped cream & ice cream (€7.90/€6.50)
- Free Snorkmaiden paw decoration with face painting purchase
- Full event details: [moominworld.fi/programme-and-events](https://www.moominworld.fi/programme-and-events/)

### Must-See Attractions (Toddler-Tested)

**🏠 Moominhouse** — The iconic house! Kids can explore from the attic to the jam cellar. The 4yo will love this.

**🎭 Theatre Emma** — Main show "Snorkmaiden presents: Grande Cabaré"
- Performances at 12:00, 13:00, 14:00, 15:00, 16:00
- Available in Finnish, Swedish, and English
- ⏱ ~20 min show, good enough for a 4yo

**🎪 Small Stage** — Multiple short shows throughout the day
- 11:30 — Little My's Morning Mischief (Finnish)
- 12:30/14:30/15:30 — Jam Adventure
- 13:30 — Moomin Family Singing Moment (music, no language barrier!)
- Perfect for short attention spans

**🤖 Snork's Park of Inventions** — Puppet theatre with "Snork's Space Adventure"
- Showings at 12:00, 13:00, 15:00, 16:00
- Great for the 4yo

**🌸 Alice's Herb Garden** — "Alice's Playtime" at 12:00, 13:00, 15:30
- Perfect for the 2yo — gentle, interactive play

**🎵 Snufkin's Camp** — Storytelling at 13:45, 14:45, 15:45
- Available in Finnish and Swedish

**🏖 Beach** — Yes, there's a beach at Moominworld! Bring swimsuits.
- 💡 The 2yo will love splashing in the shallow water

**🎨 Snorkmaiden's Beauty Salon** — Face painting (with special Midsummer paw decoration)

**🎠 Playground areas** — Multiple play areas scattered throughout the island

### Tips for Toddlers

- **Arrive early** (opens 10:00) to beat crowds and see shows first run
- **Magic bracelet** at entrance — good for keeping track of kids
- **Stroller-friendly** paths throughout the island
- **Restaurant** on-site with kids' menu
- **Sniff's Shop** for souvenirs
- **Pappan's Foto** — professional family photos with Moomin characters
- Ticket from ~€30–43, book online for best price — [moominworld.fi/tickets-and-prices](https://www.moominworld.fi/tickets-and-prices/)
- Opening times: [moominworld.fi/opening-times](https://www.moominworld.fi/opening-times/)

### Afternoon/Evening

- After Moominworld, explore **[Naantali Old Town](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/naantali-old-town)** — beautiful cobblestone streets, colorful wooden houses
- **[Naantali beach](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/naantali-beach)** — one of Finland's most famous beaches, great for evening play
- Dinner in Naantali — Merisali restaurant recommended for fresh seafood

## Day 3 — Sunday June 21: Turku Return & More Fun

### Morning: Active Play

- **[Seikkailupuisto (Adventure Park)](https://www.turku.fi/lastenkulttuurikeskus-fari/seikkailupuisto)** — Turku's best playground at Kupittaa! Always open, free entry.
  - Mushroom shower (water feature) 🚿
  - Paddling stream through the playground in summer
  - Water play area with changing facilities nearby
  - Climbing structures, swings, trampoline, stone castle for the 4yo
  - Sand areas
  - Completely FREE
  - 📍 Kupittaankatu 2
  - 💡 This is a MUST — best playground in Turku for toddlers
  - More info: [visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/kupittaa-park](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/kupittaa-park)

- **[Kupittaa Bird Park](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/kupittaa-park)** — Right next to Seikkailupuisto. Kids can see birds up close. Very peaceful.

### Late Morning

- **[Forum Marinum](http://www.forum-marinum.fi/en/)** — Maritime museum with interactive exhibits. Has a large boat collection (Suomen Joutsen, Bore, etc.) and hands-on displays. The 4yo will enjoy the interactive parts. Summer season includes access to museum ships.
  - 📍 Linnankatu 72 | ⏰ Check [forum-marinum.fi](http://www.forum-marinum.fi/en/) for summer hours
  - 💰 From €14 (check website for current prices)
  - Visit Turku info: [visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/forum-marinum](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/forum-marinum)
  - 💡 More engaging for the 4yo; 2yo might prefer the outdoor ship areas

### Lunch

- Back to **[Turun Kauppahalli](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g189949-d3164688-Reviews-Turun_Kauppahalli-Turku_Southwest_Finland.html)** or Market Square area food stalls

### Afternoon: More Exploration

- **[Turku Cathedral](https://www.turuntuomiokirkko.fi/english)** — Currently under renovation (until Christmas 2028), but the exterior and 101m tower are impressive. Quick photo stop.
  - 📍 Tuomiokirkonkatu 9 | 💰 Free to view exterior

- **[Aboa Vetus Ars Nova](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/aboa-vetus-ars-nova)** — Archaeology museum built on top of ancient ruins. You can see the actual medieval foundations through glass floors. Fascinating concept but might be more adult-focused. Skip if kids are tired.
  - 📍 Vapaudenkatu 1–3

- **[Turku City Library](https://en.visitturku.fi/kohdekortit/turku-city-library)** — Beautiful building with a dedicated children's section. Great rainy-day backup.
  - 📍 Linnanmaa 6

### Final Afternoon

- **[Ispoinen Beach](https://www.suomi.fi/services/servicepoint/ispoinen-beach-city-of-turku/438d4ed9-8df5-4025-9667-fb6449e940af)** or **[Saaronniemi (Ruissalo)](https://www.suomi.fi/services/servicepoint/saaronniemi-beach-city-of-turku/9a1e5b31-df7f-4916-8da4-0dab4055debb)** return visit — great for a relaxed Sunday afternoon with the kids
- **Ice cream** at the [Market Square](https://www.turku.fi/en/families-children) — a proper trip finale 🍦

## Practical Tips

### For the 2yo
- Stroller is very useful, especially at Moominworld
- Nap-friendly: Seikkailupuisto has quiet corners, Moominworld has peaceful spots
- Beach/shallow water will be the biggest hit
- Alice's Herb Garden playtime is specifically toddler-friendly

### For the 4yo
- Can handle most shows and attractions
- The glassblowing, Moomin shows, and Forum Marinum ships will be highlights
- Can participate in outdoor games at Moominworld

### General
- **Weather:** Late June in Turku = 15–22°C, long daylight (sunset ~10 PM). Pack layers.
- **Insect repellent** — midges can be active in June
- **Swimsuits** for both kids (beach + Moominworld)
- **Sunscreen** — Finnish sun is stronger than you'd expect
- **Cash + card** — most places take cards, but some market stalls prefer cash
- **[Föli app](https://foli.fi/en/)** for public transport if not driving

### Packing Checklist
- [ ] Swimsuits (×2) + towels
- [ ] Sunscreen + hats
- [ ] Insect repellent
- [ ] Stroller (collapsible)
- [ ] Change of clothes (×2 per kid, for beach/water)
- [ ] Snacks + water bottles
- [ ] Small first aid (plasters etc.)
- [ ] Camera/phone for Moominworld photos
